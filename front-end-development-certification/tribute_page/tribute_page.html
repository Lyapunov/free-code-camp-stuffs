Mini biography:

Paul Verhoeven is best known as the director of Basic Instinct, Total Recall and Robocop. Born in Amsterdam in 1938. After finishing his studies, he pursued his career in Holland by directing several feature films. The international success of his films encouraged him to settle in the United States in 1985 and he became one of Hollywood's blockbuster directors. Turkish Delight won a "best film of the century" award in a popular vote, and his 1977 World War II film Soldier of Orange received a Golden Globe nomination.
